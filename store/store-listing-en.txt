== Display temperature and status of Luxtronik 2.0 heat pump, heating and water

Display temperature and status of heating and hot water of a heat pump controlled by the alpha innotec Luxtronik 2.0 controller.

You must be connected to the same network as the controller (so for remote access you may use a VPN connection into your home network).

You can currently only display values, not change settings.
